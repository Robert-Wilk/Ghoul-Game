Ghoul Game!

A hackathon project by Robert Wilk and Nick Jackson

Welcome to Ghoul Game! This is a short game that plays similar to Black Jack.

The goal of the game is to keep adding to your score while being cautious of The Ghoul

  • There are 2 buttons: Add, Take:
  • Add: Add 1 point to your total score
  • Finish: Take your current score and end the game

When you add to your score, there is a random chance that The Ghoul will jump out and take all of your points!
Highscores get recorded to the leaderboard!
